What they'll learn

Every game, every reveal, every fact traces back to one of these 11 core principles.

1
💰

Pay Yourself First

Save before you spend. The first dollar of every paycheck has your name on it.

2
🚀

Compound Growth

Make your money work for you. Compound growth is the closest thing to magic in finance.

3
🧾

Wealth Is the Gap

Spend less than you earn. The bigger the gap, the faster wealth builds.

4
🔍

Know What You Own

Invest in what you understand. Know how a company actually makes money.

5
🧺

Diversify

Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Spread risk across many things.

6
⏳

Be Patient

Think in years, not days. Markets reward people who can sit still.

7
🧘

Control Your Emotions

Don't follow the crowd. The best decisions feel boring at the time.

8
🏛️

Think Like an Owner

Stocks are real businesses. Buying a share = owning a slice of a company.

9
📆

Stay Consistent

Regular investing beats perfect timing. Same amount, every month, for years.

10
🏷️

Price vs Value

Expensive isn't always valuable. The price is what you pay; value is what you get.

11
🏠

Own Assets

Make money while you sleep. Own things that pay you — not just stuff that sits.
